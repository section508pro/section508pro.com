Public Documents

According to the official Section 508 Report to Congress and the President, 68% of federal downloadable PDFs do not meet Section 508 legal requirements. Only 23% of federal government PDFs available to the public meet Section 508 legal requirements. Section 508 Pro helps track and manage your document remediation process.
