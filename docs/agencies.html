Federal Agencies

All US Federal agencies are legally required, by Section 508 of the Rehabilitation Act to provide accessible websites. When 1 in 4 adults in the US have some type of disability, the vital importance of this law becomes clear - a government "of the people, by the people, for the people" includes all people, including those with disabilities.

On August 8th, 2023 we visited 8,724 federal web pages. We found 66,512 accessibility errors on those pages.
